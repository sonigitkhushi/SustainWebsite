Sustainability

A sustainability website is a platform that provides information and resources related to sustainable practices and solutions.

  • The main goal of a sustainability website is to raise awareness and educate people on the importance of sustainability,.
  • Sustainability websites often feature articles, blog posts, and news updates on the latest developments in the field of sustainability.
  • They may also provide resources such as calculators, guides, and toolkits to help individuals, organizations, and communities take action towards sustainability.

a sustainability website is a valuable resource for anyone looking to learn about and take action on sustainability issues. By providing education, resources, and opportunities for engagement, these websites play an important role in promoting sustainable practices and solutions.

Understanding Your Digital Footprint

An organization’s digital footprint grows as it produces content, launches websites, shares marketing updates, hosts virtual workshops, upgrades cloud accounts, and designs more apps to meet evolving business needs. As an example:

  • 01 Active digital footprints

    An active digital footprint is where the user has deliberately shared information about themselves – for example, through posting or participating on social networking sites or online forums. If a user is logged into a website through a registered username or profile, any posts they make form part of their active digital footprint.

  • 02 Passive digital footprints
